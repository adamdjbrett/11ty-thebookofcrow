<h1 id="about">about crow</h1>

<p>uncle crow is the curious force behind this field guide. i collect hard-won notes about everyday carry, cigar box guitars, and all the linux terminal tricks that keep my gear running. i like tools that are durable, repairable, and easy to pass along.</p>

<h2 id="approach">approach</h2>
<p>simplicity stays at the center. if the tools are inexpensive and replicable, anyone can learn them. the Book of Crow documents the mindset that favors resourcefulness over showmanship.</p>
